THE HISTORY

×
      The Bachelor of Science in Mathematics major in Computer Science program at URSM underwent a comprehensive feasibility study, spearheaded by the Computer Science faculty. The study encompassed surveys conducted across Morong, Baras, Tanay, Pililla, and Teresa, focusing on zoning considerations. Presentations were made to the Academic Council at the college level, specifically at the Morong campus, garnering approval from the Board of Regents. Following necessary revisions, the program received final approval on March 26, 2021. Currently, accreditation is pending, with plans for implementation and activities to engage both faculty and students in the field of Computer Science. Furthermore, initiatives include engagement with TESDA's Computer Systems Servicing program, emphasizing networking. Job opportunities aligned with the program's focus are being explored, with a faculty consisting of five members with relevant expertise.
